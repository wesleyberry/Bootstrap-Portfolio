About Me

My name is Wesley Berry, and I am an aspiring software developer. I grew up in Wimberley, Tx and am currently located in San Marcos. I spend a healthy yet large majority of my time working on my education at the University of Texas' Coding Bootcamp. I am developing and refining my skills in web development to adequately prepare myself for a career. I enjoy expressing myself creatively, and coding is the best means by which to do so.

In my limited free time, you can find me in the gym, meal prepping, or playing music with my friends. I also enjoy spending time outdoors, whether it be hiking, playing frisbee, or running a Spartan Race.
